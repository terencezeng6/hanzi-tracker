About

This is a simple tool I made while I was in China to track the status of characters I'm learning. Users can log in with a Google account to save their data.

The characters are taken from the government's 通用规范汉字表 (List of Commonly Used Chinese Characters). They're sorted by frequency, from most common to least common, and the frequency data is taken from Jun Da's character frequency list.

It's pretty barebones right now, but I'm hoping to add more features — let me know if you have suggestions!
